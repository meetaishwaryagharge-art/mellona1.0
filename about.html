Welcome To Mellona

We Are A Global Trading And Sourcing Company, Providing Customized Solutions To Buyers Around The World. We Specialize In Sourcing And Producing Goods As Per Client Requirements, Ensuring Quality And Reliability Throughout The Process.

One Of Our Key Initiatives, Mellona Foods, Is Dedicated To Promoting High-Quality Food Products. This Venture Supports Farmers And Small-Scale Food Businesses By Helping Them Access Wider Markets, Improve Their Livelihoods, And Deliver Pure, Trustworthy Food To Consumers.

OUR MISSION

To source and trade quality products globally while supporting farmers and small food businesses with better market access.

OUR VISION

To be a trusted name in global sourcing and trading.

Who We Are?

We are a passionate team of young professionals united by a shared vision to become a global leader in food trading. Driven by our belief in India’s rich legacy of spices and diverse food culture, we see food export as a vital contributor to the nation’s growth. Specializing in the export of Indian spices and other food products, we also bring deep market insights and a continuously updated global food trade database to support our clients with informed, strategic decisions.

What We Do?

We specialize in the export of premium Indian spices, but our services go far beyond that. Recognizing the global demand for authentic Indian products, we also offer the export of organic foods, millets, and a variety of high-quality food items. From identifying the right buyers to ensuring secure packaging and smooth shipping, we provide end-to-end support to make your export journey hassle-free. Whether your destination is Europe, the Middle East, the Americas, or anywhere else in the world — we make sure your products reach with care and commitment.
